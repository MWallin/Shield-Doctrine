# The Stellar Republic - Locations

## Document Summary

This document catalogs notable planets, cities, and locations within the Stellar Republic's 42 star systems. Locations are organized by system and include relevant details for worldbuilding consistency.

## Core Systems (Capital Region)

### Terra Nova System (Capital)

**Roma Stellaris** (Capital City, Terra Nova Prime):
- Population: ~15 billion
- Founded: 2165 CE (first settlement after exodus)
- Character: Gleaming federal capital, seat of Senate and Federal Consuls
- Notable features: Federal Forum, Senate House, Consular Palace, Federal Archives
- Architecture: Neo-Roman revival with modern materials
- Demographics: Highly diverse, ~35% Full Citizens, 55% Resident Citizens, 10% Non-Citizens

**Portus Magnus** (Major port city, Terra Nova Prime):
- Population: ~8 billion
- Character: Primary spaceport and commercial hub
- Notable features: Orbital elevators, massive cargo facilities, shipyards
- Economy: Trade, logistics, ship construction

## Major Systems (Settlement Age 2150-2300)

### Meridian System

**New Alexandria** (Meridian III):
- Population: ~12 billion
- Founded: 2195 CE
- Character: University city, scientific and cultural center
- Notable features: Federal University, Great Library, Research Parks
- Known for: Academic excellence, arts, philosophy
- Demographics: High concentration of Scientific Track and educators

**Portus Secundus** (Meridian II):
- Population: ~5 billion
- Character: Industrial manufacturing center
- Economy: Heavy industry, ship components, advanced manufacturing

---

## Mid-Era Systems (Settlement Age 2300-2500)

### Concordia System

**Nova Carthago** (Concordia Prime):
- Population: ~9 billion
- Founded: 2348 CE
- Character: Major commercial and trading hub
- Notable features: Grand Bazaar, Merchant Guilds, Banking District
- Economy: Finance, trade, commercial services
- Known for: Entrepreneurial culture, business innovation

**Valentia** (Concordia Prime - northern continent):
- Population: ~3 billion
- Character: Smaller city known for Vigiles training facilities
- Notable features: Federal Vigiles Academy, Criminal Justice Center
- Notable residents: Former detective **Dr. Marcus Hadrian** worked here for 6 years before transferring to Scientific Track
- Known for: Law enforcement excellence, detective training programs
- Crime rate: Moderate (major port city = organized crime presence)

---

## Frontier Systems (Settlement Age 2500-2700)

### Fortuna System

**Portus Fortunae** (Fortuna II):
- Population: ~2 billion
- Founded: 2587 CE
- Character: Frontier mining and refining center
- Economy: Asteroid mining, resource extraction, refining
- Demographics: Transient population, many non-citizens seeking opportunity

---

## Research Stations & Outposts

### Obsidian Station (Frontier Region)

**Location:** Obsidian System (frontier, ~70 light-years from Terra Nova)
- Type: Deep space research station
- Population: ~2,500 (pre-attack)
- Founded: 2735 CE
- Purpose: Study planetary formation, exotic materials, artifact research
- Notable: Dr. James Adeyemi's planetary geology research base
- Status: Destroyed in Krell attack, 2750 CE
- Planet Below: Rocky terrestrial planet with volcanic activity, no native life

## Historical Notes

**Naming Conventions:**
- Core systems: Roman/Latin names (Terra Nova, Roma Stellaris)
- Early settlements: Classical references (New Alexandria, Nova Carthago)
- Later systems: Virtue/hope themes (Concordia, Fortuna)
- Ports: "Portus" prefix common (Magnus, Secundus, Fortunae)

**Population Distribution:**
- Core systems (10): 150-180 billion (60-70% of total)
- Mid-era systems (20): 60-90 billion (25-35% of total)
- Frontier systems (12): 10-30 billion (5-10% of total)

**City Population Scale:**
- Mega-cities: 10+ billion (Roma Stellaris, New Alexandria)
- Major cities: 5-10 billion (Portus Magnus, Nova Carthago)
- Regional cities: 1-5 billion (Valentia, Portus Fortunae)
- Small cities: 100 million - 1 billion
- Towns/settlements: <100 million
