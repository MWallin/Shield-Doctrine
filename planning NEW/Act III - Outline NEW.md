- How has Shield Doctrine scaled up?
- What's changed politically/militarily?
- How have characters grown?
- Manufacturing and training expansion?

**Hammerfall Operation:**
- Multi-fleet coordination
- 24 Javelin teams operating independently
- Phase-anchor technology (experimental, 75% success rate)
- Incomplete preparation forcing adaptation
- Professional competence theme in action

**Payoff & Resolution:**
- All major plot threads resolve
- Character arcs complete
- Thematic statements fulfilled
- Citizenship reforms implemented
- Lessons learned institutionalized

**Balancing Act:**
- Victory but with costs (136 KIA, Captain Park's sacrifice)
- Success despite incomplete prep
- Validation of themes (competence, adaptation, sacrifice)
- Not too perfect, not too dark
